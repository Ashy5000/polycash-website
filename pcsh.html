PCSH


PCSH is the native cryptocurrency token of the Polycash ecosystem. It can be used to pay for transaction fees, deployment costs, and data storage. In the active testnet, there are currently around 10 PCSH minted. To aquire PCSH, you can purchase it from someone else or earn it through the Polycash Incentives System, contributing computing power to secure the network and earn rewards.

Right now, the PCSH available is testnet PCSH. When the Polycash mainnet is deployed, tokens will not transfer over. If you have testnet tokens at the time of deployment, it is recommended to sell them to purchase mainnet tokens.
